The Best Movies Ever According to Brandon Dilts

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Alien

One of the best horror sci-fi movies ever made with awesome animitronics and set design. No movie has better atmosphere

Predator

The best qualities of action, horror, thriller, sci-fi movies all in one film. With practical effects that still are impressive to this day. Incredibly entertaining, and endlessly quotable.

Nightmare on Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors

My favorite slasher changes a lot between many films, but I always find time to rewatch arguably the strongest entry in the elm street franchise. Likable characters, entertaining deaths, and Freddy is at his best.
